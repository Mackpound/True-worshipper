    OUR RHYTHM IS TO GATHER TOGETHER TO WORSHIP AND SCATTER TO SERVE

    We are a Jesus church. A small tribe of His followers connected by our common faith and a deep desire to see our city [and the world] come to know His power and beauty.

    We gather each Sunday and there is a spot for everyone of all ages to join us for church. Read below to learn more about what to expect at our 515 Location.

    When Do We Gather?

    • We gather on Sundays at 9:30 am and 11:45 am! We are located at Ikorodu

    Is There A Place For My Kids?

    • bloom + True worshipper Kids are the rooms of our House designed for your kids! bloom (6 Weeks – Pre-K) and Passion Kids (K-5th) both meet in the same building as our gatherings. We have the best Door Holders that will help direct you where to go, get your kids checked in, and take great care of your kids while you attend the gathering. Check-in opens as early as 8:45 am, and you can plan to pick up your child after the gathering ends. Middle School Students (6th-8th) will meet in the Loading Dock at 11:45 am. If you have a High School Student you can learn more about their gathering times Here

    Where Do I Park?

    • We will have Door Holders ready to help you find a parking spot in our lot. We do have limited parking, but once our lot is full you can park at Rollins – 2170 Piedmont Rd NE, Atlanta, GA 30324 with a short walk or shuttle ride to our front doors. For our bloom + Passion Kids families, please use your flashers so our team can direct you well!

    We can’t wait to see you this Sunday!
