    Global founder Experience

    Founding True Worshippers Outreach and Evangelical Ministry: Giving life a meaning

    In the heart of True Worshippers Church, the radiant spirit of Sister Merit Chidison, the church's founder, continues to illuminate the path of countless believers. With a vision that transcended the ordinary, Sister Merit embarked on a remarkable journey to establish a spiritual haven that would come to be known as True Worshippers.

    The seed of inspiration was planted in Sister Merit's heart during a period of deep reflection and prayer. In the late 1980s, sensing a divine calling, she envisioned a place where people from all walks of life could gather to worship authentically and build a community grounded in love and faith. With unwavering determination and a profound sense of purpose, Sister Merit took the first steps towards realizing her vision.

    True Worshippers Church began humbly, with a small group gathering in Sister Merit's living room. Her passion for inclusivity and authentic worship quickly attracted like-minded individuals, and the congregation began to grow. Sister Merit's dynamic leadership and nurturing spirit fostered an environment where everyone felt welcomed, valued, and encouraged to explore their faith.

    The church's ethos, encapsulated in the phrase "Moving from Death to Life," became not just a slogan but a lived experience under Sister Merit's guidance. Baptism ceremonies took on a special significance, symbolizing not only a spiritual rebirth but also a commitment to a transformative journey led by the teachings of Christ.

    Sister Merit's leadership extended beyond the church walls, as she initiated various community outreach programs. True Worshippers became known not only for its vibrant worship services but also for its dedication to serving the broader community. Whether it was providing assistance to those in need or fostering unity through interfaith dialogues, Sister Merit's vision embraced the holistic well-being of individuals and the community.

    As the church continued to grow, so did Sister Merit's vision. True Worshippers became a beacon of hope, a place where people found solace in times of adversity and joy in moments of celebration. Sister Merit's emphasis on fostering genuine connections within the congregation contributed to the church's unique atmosphere, where everyone felt like part of an extended spiritual family.

    Reflecting on her journey, Sister Merit shares, "Establishing True Worshippers has been a testament to God's grace and the power of community. Witnessing lives transformed and hearts renewed has been the most rewarding aspect of this journey."

    Today, True Worshippers Church stands as a testament to Sister Merit Chidison's enduring legacy. Her founding vision of a vibrant, inclusive, and faith-driven community continues to thrive. Sister Merit's commitment to "Moving from Death to Life" echoes in every baptismal ceremony, every heartfelt prayer, and every act of kindness within the church and the wider community.

    As the founder of True Worshippers, Sister Merit Chidison remains an inspiration, demonstrating that a vision fueled by faith and love has the power to transform lives and build a community that reflects the very essence of true worship.

    Local pastor Experience

    Shepherd of Faith: Sister Glory Nwaechefu's Journey as the Local Pastor of True Worshippers Outreach and Evangelical Ministry

    In the heart of True Worshippers Outreach and Evangelical Ministry, Sister Glory Nwaechefu stands as a beacon of spiritual guidance, leading the congregation on a transformative journey of faith. As the local pastor, Sister Glory's commitment to the church's mission is woven into the very fabric of the ministry, creating a nurturing environment where hearts are touched, lives are transformed, and the message of God's love resonates.

    Sister Glory's journey as the local pastor began with a deep sense of divine calling. Her passion for evangelism and her unwavering belief in the power of outreach propelled her to take on the responsibilities of shepherding the flock. True Worshippers, under her leadership, has become more than a place of worship; it has evolved into a dynamic ministry with a mission to reach hearts far beyond the church walls.

    The ministry's outreach efforts, inspired by Sister Glory's vision, extend into the community, embracing the marginalized and sharing the transformative message of Christ's love. From organizing community events to spearheading charitable initiatives, Sister Glory exemplifies the ministry's commitment to embodying the teachings of compassion and service.

    One of the hallmarks of True Worshippers Outreach and Evangelical Ministry under Sister Glory's leadership is the emphasis on personal connection. Sister Glory believes in the power of building relationships within the congregation and the broader community. Her pastoral approach involves active listening, offering guidance, and creating a space where individuals feel seen and heard in their spiritual journey.

    Baptism ceremonies, a central facet of the ministry, take on a profound meaning under Sister Glory's guidance. The phrase "Moving from Death to Life" resonates not only as a symbolic act during the baptismal rites but also as a lived experience within the hearts of the congregation. Sister Glory's words during these ceremonies echo the ministry's commitment to ushering believers into a life transformed by the redemptive power of faith.

    Reflecting on her role, Sister Glory shares, "Being the local pastor at True Worshippers Outreach and Evangelical Ministry is a humbling experience. It's about leading by example, walking alongside each member in their faith journey, and witnessing the beautiful tapestry of lives woven together by God's grace."

    Sister Glory's leadership extends beyond the pulpit, as she encourages members to actively engage in the ministry's various programs, from Bible studies to community outreach initiatives. True Worshippers becomes not just a place of worship but a community actively involved in spreading the message of hope, love, and redemption.

    The transformative impact of Sister Glory's leadership is evident in the testimonies of the congregation. Members speak of finding renewed purpose, experiencing healing, and forging deeper connections with both God and fellow believers. True Worshippers Outreach and Evangelical Ministry, under Sister Glory's pastoral guidance, becomes a living testament to the transformative power of faith and community.

    In the heart of Sister Glory Nwaechefu, the local pastor, True Worshippers Outreach and Evangelical Ministry thrives as a place where faith comes alive, lives are changed, and the message of God's boundless love reverberates far beyond the sanctuary. Sister Glory's journey is a testament to the profound impact that a dedicated shepherd can have on a community, fostering a space where individuals truly experience the journey "Moving from Death to Life."
